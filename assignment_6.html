Find your next job at UK

Competitive compensation and benefits

  • Outstanding health, dental and vision insurance
  • 200% match on your retirement contributions
  • Free tuition for employees and tuition discounts for dependents
  • Access to well-being specialists for mental health, physical activity, personal finance, healthy nutrition and much more
  • Generous paid time off, including 13 paid holidays per year, 10 to 15 days of paid vacation per year initially, paid sick leave, two well-being days per year, two weeks of paid parental leave, one week of paid elder care leave

Strong Communities Creating Strong Students

Through Living Learning Communities and Residential Colleges UK offers students the opportunity to live and learn together in integrated academic residential environments. This dynamic residential experience offers specialized programming, interactions with UK faculty and staff, and a supportive community that focuses on student success.

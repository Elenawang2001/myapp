:::::: Starting in the second semester of 2008 academic year, the SIIT Activity Transcript system has been developed by SIIT Student Affairs Division under the supervision of Associate Professor Dr. Ruengsak Kawtummachai, the Assistant Director for Student Affairs of Academic Years 2006-2008. The system has continued to be developed under the supervision of Associate Professor Dr. Chalie Charoenlarpnopparut, the Assistant Director for Student Affairs of Academic Years 2009-now.
:::::: The SIIT Activity Transcript Systemis designed to encourage SIIT students to organize and/or participate in student activities. The activities are extracurricular and are focused on provision of useful experiences for students. Moreover, "friendships & unity" among students is also strengthened through the activities.

Cancellation


of Using the "SIIT Student Activity Records Book"
In order to improve our SIIT Activity Transcript System, the "SIIT Student Activity Records Book" is not required to be attached when a student requests an official "SIIT Activity Transcript", from March 2011 onwards.
In addition, we strongly recommend that students must sign-up/register (paper-based and/or web-based) when organizing/participating in an activity.
Announced by SIIT Student Affairs Division 18 March 2011
